Father and Son

A Bond Built on Faith, Love and Divine Purpose

Some relationships begin at birth, but others begin long before a child enters the world. The story of my relationship with my son, Munachimso, is one of those special stories that began even before I first held him in my arms.

Long before his birth, while his mother was still carrying him and I was preparing for my Master's examination in Public Health, God gave me a dream that would remain forever engraved in my heart. In that dream, the name "Munachimso" was revealed to me.

At the time, I did not fully understand the significance of what I had seen, but the name stayed with me. As the months passed and our son continued to grow in the womb, I became increasingly convinced that this was not merely a dream but a divine message. When he was eventually born, there was no doubt in my heart that the name belonged to him.

The name Munachimso, meaning "God is with me", perfectly captures the story of my life and the story of his beginning. It serves as a daily reminder that God's presence has never departed from us and that His hand has been upon our family through every season.

A Name That Connects Two Generations

Alongside the name Munachimso, we also gave him the baptismal name Emmanuel. Interestingly, Emmanuel is also my own baptismal name. Although we chose the name partly because our son was born during the month of December—a season that celebrates the birth of Christ—the connection became even more meaningful because it linked father and son through a shared name and a shared faith.

While I was born in June and he was born in December, the name Emmanuel continues to remind us of God's promise to be with His people. In many ways, it symbolizes a spiritual connection between us that will remain throughout our lives.

A Living Testimony of God's Faithfulness

To me, the birth of Munachimso represents much more than the arrival of a son. His birth is a summary of how far God has brought me in life.

Every challenge overcome, every prayer answered, every disappointment turned into a testimony, every achievement attained, and every step of my journey finds meaning when I look at him. He reminds me daily that God has been faithful.

When I look into his eyes, I do not only see my son; I see evidence of God's goodness. I see a continuation of a story that God began many years ago and a future that God Himself is already preparing.

Just as God has led me through the different stages of my life, I trust that He will also guide Munachimso, protect him, strengthen him and lead him into the glorious future prepared for him.

Our Special Bond

Although Munachimso is only six months old, the bond we share is something truly special. There are moments when words fail to describe the joy I feel simply watching him smile, laugh or stretch out his little hands towards me.

One of the most beautiful things about him is his love for togetherness. Whether he is with his mother, with me, or with both of us, he seems happiest when we are together as a family.

He enjoys our playtime immensely. His face lights up whenever we laugh, talk, play or spend time together. Sometimes all it takes is a simple game, a smile, or a gentle conversation for him to burst into excitement.

As his father, there is no greater feeling than knowing that my presence brings him comfort and happiness. Every moment spent carrying him, playing with him, or watching him discover the world is a treasure I hold dearly in my heart.

My Prayer for My Son

My beloved son, Munachimso Emmanuel, you are one of God's greatest gifts to me. Long before you were born, God knew you. Long before I carried you in my arms, He carried you in His plans.

My prayer is that you will grow to know the God who revealed your name before your birth. May you walk in His wisdom, enjoy His favour, and fulfill His purpose for your life.

May your future be brighter than my past, your opportunities greater than mine, your achievements higher than mine, and your impact upon the world far beyond anything I could ever imagine.

Whenever life presents challenges, may you remember the meaning of your name: Munachimso — God is with me. May that truth guide you, strengthen you and accompany you throughout your entire journey on earth.

A Father's Promise

As long as God grants me life, I promise to love you, support you, guide you, pray for you and celebrate you. I may not always have all the answers, but I will always be your father, your supporter and one of your biggest believers.

You are not merely my son. You are a testimony, a blessing, a prayer answered, a dream fulfilled and a reminder that God's plans are always beautiful.

"My son, you were first revealed in a dream, then carried in prayer, and now you are carried in love."

With endless love from Dad.
