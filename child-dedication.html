Munachimso's Child Dedication & Baptism

A Special Day of Faith, Thanksgiving and Divine Blessings

One of the most memorable and significant moments in the life of our beloved son, Munachimso, was his Child Dedication and Baptism, which took place on the 15th day of March, 2026.

On this special day, we gathered in the presence of God at Christ the King Catholic Church (CKC), Awka, to publicly present our son before the Lord in thanksgiving and to seek God's continued guidance, protection and blessings upon his life. It was a day filled with joy, gratitude and deep spiritual significance for our family.

A Sacred Beginning

During the church service, Munachimso was baptized and dedicated to God, marking the beginning of his spiritual journey in the Christian faith. Through the sacrament of Baptism, he was welcomed into the family of Christ, and through his dedication, we committed ourselves as parents to nurture him in the ways of God, teaching him the values of faith, love, humility and service to humanity.

As we stood before the congregation, our hearts were filled with thanksgiving for God's faithfulness throughout the pregnancy, his safe delivery, and the many blessings we have continued to enjoy since his birth.

A Celebration Shared with Loved Ones

The occasion was graced by a number of our close friends and family members who were able to join us in celebrating this important milestone. Though the gathering was modest, it was rich in love, prayers, encouragement and goodwill. Their presence made the occasion even more meaningful and memorable.

We remain sincerely grateful to everyone who attended, prayed with us, and shared in our joy. Their support and fellowship added warmth and beauty to the celebration and reminded us of the importance of family, friendship and community.

Our Prayer for Munachimso

As parents, our greatest desire is that Munachimso will grow in God's wisdom, love and favour. We pray that the grace received on the day of his Baptism and Dedication will continually guide his steps and shape his future.

May he grow to become a man of integrity, compassion, wisdom and faith. May God's protection remain upon him throughout his life, and may he fulfill the beautiful purpose for which he was created.

A Day We Will Always Cherish

The 15th of March, 2026, will forever occupy a special place in our hearts. It was not merely a ceremony but a declaration of our faith, our gratitude to God, and our commitment to raising Munachimso in a loving and God-centered home.

As we reflect on that beautiful day, we are reminded that children are indeed a gift from God, entrusted into our care for a season. We thank Him for the privilege of being Munachimso's parents and for the countless blessings that continue to accompany his life.
