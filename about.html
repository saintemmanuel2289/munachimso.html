About Us

Our story is one of love, friendship, commitment, and God's abundant blessings. What began as a beautiful relationship blossomed into a lifelong journey of companionship and family.

On the 5th of January 2025, the first Sunday of the year, we joyfully celebrated our traditional marriage in the presence of family, friends, and loved ones. It was a memorable occasion filled with happiness, laughter, prayers, and the rich cultural heritage that united our families.

Our wedding celebrations were made even more special by the presence of distinguished guests, family members, friends, and well-wishers who came to rejoice with us. We were particularly honored by the attendance of Hon. Ossy Onuko, PhD, the Managing Director and Chief Executive Officer of the Awka Capital Territory Development Authority (ACTDA), and his beloved wife, Pharmacist Oby Onuko. Their presence added prestige and joy to our memorable occasion.

We were also delighted to welcome Dr. Egurefa Samson, a respected lecturer and Head of Department at Delta State University, whose support and goodwill greatly enriched our celebration. Alongside these notable personalities were numerous relatives, colleagues, neighbors, and friends who traveled from far and near to share in our happiness. Their prayers, encouragement, gifts, and expressions of love made our wedding ceremony truly unforgettable.

Looking back, we remain deeply grateful to everyone who stood with us during this important chapter of our lives. The overwhelming love and support we received served as a beautiful reminder that our union was not only celebrated by us but also embraced by a community of wonderful people whose presence and kindness will forever remain in our hearts.

Just one month later, on the 5th of February 2025, we solemnized our union through a court wedding, publicly affirming our commitment to love, honor, and support one another through every season of life. That special day marked the beginning of a new chapter as husband and wife, united by love and a shared vision for the future.

As the year unfolded, God blessed our home with an even greater gift. On the 1st day of December 2025, we welcomed our beloved son, Munachimso, into the world. His arrival brought immeasurable joy, renewed purpose, and countless reasons to smile. From the moment we held him in our arms, our hearts were filled with gratitude and a deeper appreciation for the beauty of family.

Today, we continue to build our home on the foundations of love, faith, mutual respect, understanding, and unwavering support for one another. Every day presents an opportunity to create new memories, celebrate life's blessings, and strengthen the bond that unites us as a family.

Our journey has been filled with precious moments, and we remain thankful for the grace that has guided us every step of the way. Together, we look forward to many more years of happiness, growth, laughter, and cherished family experiences.

Welcome to our story. Welcome to our family.
